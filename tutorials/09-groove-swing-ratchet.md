# 09 Groove Swing Ratchet

Goal: make the clock itself expressive.

Patch: [examples/groove_ratchet_arp.pulse](/Users/md/Downloads/plugin%20language/examples/groove_ratchet_arp.pulse)

Concepts:

- `swing` shifts alternating pulses.
- `ratchet` creates extra triggers inside a step.
- `groove` applies a repeating timing profile.

What to try:

1. Push swing above `60%`.
2. Change `ratchet 3` to `ratchet 2` or `4`.
3. Rewrite the groove pattern.

Key idea:

In Fabric, timing is part of composition. The clock is not just a boring metronome.
