# 11 Temporal Smear

Goal: blend the present note with the recent past.

Patch: [examples/fibonacci_smear.pulse](/Users/md/Downloads/plugin%20language/examples/fibonacci_smear.pulse)

Concepts:

- `memory smear` stores recent pitch history.
- weighted blending produces harmonic afterimages.
- a generator feeding smear can become richer and less literal.

What to try:

1. Change the smear weights.
2. Increase the amount of drift.
3. Put smear after live input instead of after a generator.

Key idea:

This is one of Fabric's memory-based composition tools: the past can stay audible inside the present.
