# 16 Held Note Random

Goal: use the notes you are physically holding as the source material for a random walk.

Patch: [examples/random_held_notes.pulse](/Users/md/Downloads/plugin%20language/examples/random_held_notes.pulse)

Concepts:

- `from held notes` makes the generator listen to your live chord.
- the clock decides when the next choice happens.
- `mode walk` keeps the melody related to the held shape.

Key idea:

This is one of the best “play the system while it composes back at you” patches in Fabric.
