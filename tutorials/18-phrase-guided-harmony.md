# 18 Phrase Guided Harmony

Goal: steer harmony with a phrase plan instead of leaving every chord isolated.

Patch: [examples/phrase_guided_projector.pulse](/Users/md/Downloads/plugin%20language/examples/phrase_guided_projector.pulse)

Concepts:

- `generate phrase` or `generate progression` can push harmonic targets over time.
- `notes` can read those targets and change chord behavior.
- arrivals can feel intentional rather than accidental.

Key idea:

This is where Fabric starts behaving like a compositional system rather than a pile of effects.
