# 20 Growth And Modulator

Goal: let harmonic growth shape its own dynamics and phrasing through modulation.

Patch: [examples/growth_modulator.pulse](/Users/md/Downloads/plugin%20language/examples/growth_modulator.pulse)

Concepts:

- `generate growth` creates pitch and density.
- `shape modulator` adds evolving control over velocity, time, or gates.
- one generative layer can animate another.

Key idea:

This is a good example of Fabric’s deeper style: harmony, timing, and modulation moving together.
